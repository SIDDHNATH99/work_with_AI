# One-Day Meal Plan for Marathon Training Athlete

Here is a one-day meal plan for an athlete aiming to maximize carbohydrate loading and hydration for high-endurance marathon training:

* **Macronutrient Breakdown:** Protein (130g), Fat (80g), Carbs (480g)

## Meal Plan:

* **Breakfast (7:00 AM):** Oatmeal (100g dry weight) with honey (30g), banana (120g), and whole milk (240g). Side of whole wheat toast (50g) with almond butter (20g).

    * Protein: 5g, Fat: 8g, Carbs: 60g

* **Lunch (1:00 PM):** Grilled chicken breast (130g) with brown rice (120g dry weight), steamed sweet potato (200g), and mixed vegetables (broccoli 100g, carrots 50g). Olive oil drizzle (10g).
    * Protein: 42g, Fat: 15g, Carbs: 110g

* **Pre-Workout Snack (3:30 PM):** White bread sandwich (80g) with lean turkey (80g) and low-fat cheese (20g). Orange juice (250ml).
    * Protein: 22g, Fat: 8g, Carbs: 75g

* **Post-Workout Recovery (6:00 PM - within 30 mins of training):** Chocolate milk (400ml) with a bagel (75g) and jam (20g).
    * Protein: 18g, Fat: 8g, Carbs: 90g

* **Dinner (7:30 PM):** Baked salmon (120g) with pasta (100g dry weight), marinara sauce (100g), and roasted zucchini (150g). Side salad with olive oil vinaigrette (15g oil).
    * Protein: 35g, Fat: 18g, Carbs: 110g

* **Evening Snack (9:30 PM):** Rice cakes (40g) with honey (15g) and herbal tea (250ml).
    * Protein: 2g, Fat: 1g, Carbs: 40g
