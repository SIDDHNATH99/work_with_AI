## Email generator with AI

This project demonstrates AI email generator tool for small business owners to help them market their product

## Features
- Enter Business / Product name
- Add Details about your product
- Choose email goals and target recipients
- Choose email tone as well  

## Purpose
The goal of this experiment was to understand how ai works while creating any product or tool

## Claude Feature Used
Chat

## Learning Outcome
Claude can generate functional UI tools from structured prompts.

## Status
Built while learning Claude AI.

## Prompt used to generate the tool
Create One AI email generator which can be used by various small business owners or product owners to sell their products or to do the marketing of their product in Professional & clean way
